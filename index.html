The Nigerian Election Story

In this project, I cover Nigeria’s political history in a 3-part data visualization series.
The first part gives an overview of Nigeria’s leaders since independence in 1960. It also highlights some significant events that occurred duirng that time.
The second part examines the electoral landscape to study trends and patterns in previous elections.
The third part is a projection for the 2023 election cycle. It seeks to chart a path to victory for each of the 3 major contestants in the 2023 election cycle.
